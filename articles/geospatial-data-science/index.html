Introduction

How did we get here?

Geospatial isn’t only about geographic sciences, but also about data and information technologies — especially since the introduction of GNSS (global navigation satellite systems). However, spatial and informational technologies were often siloed. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

With today’s access to powerful tools, anyone can conduct spatial analysis from their desk — just like I did for this case study. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}

Geodatabase Project

Objective

Build a geodatabase and demonstrate a useful application or service. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}

I went on a trip to SeaWorld San Diego as my study area and collected feature layers, integrating them with an imagery basemap using ArcGIS Pro and ArcGIS Online. :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}

All data used are fictional or publicly collected for educational demonstration. :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}
